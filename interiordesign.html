Your Path to Bespoke Possession

How we protect your capital and ensure total quality from the very first consultation call up to the handover.

1. Spatial Discovery

Meet your lead spatial architect to review your lifestyle preferences, room dimensions, Vastu layouts, and design references.

2. Material Moodboarding

Review tangible swatches: hand-picked marbles, walnut veneers, anti-fingerprint laminates, custom fabrics, and smart lighting layouts.

3. 3D Render & VR walk

Explore photorealistic 3D VR renders. Refine lighting angles, texture scales, and spatial clearances before locking production blueprints.

4. German CNC Production

Blueprints are fed into our modular factory. CNC routers cut BWR plywood and hot-press laminates with zero millimeter tolerance margins.

5. Turnkey Site Installation

RCV craftsmen assemble panels dust-free, wire smart conduits, install European fitments, and apply custom low-VOC finishes.

6. Certified Handover

Conduct complete safety checks, deep modular cleaning, final accessory placement, and deliver written 10-year woodwork warranties.
