Engineering Rigor

Our QASCON-450 Quality Milestone Checks

During independent building projects, our QA managers execute rigorous laboratory and physical benchmarks at three primary phases.

Phase 01

Substructure & Foundation Concrete Lab Checks

Every batch of concrete cast undergoes compression testing (M25/M30 standards). We execute physical soil bearing audits, anti-termite treatment logs, and structural foundation rebar spacing inspections to avoid structural settling.

Phase 02

Superstructure & Masonry Structural Integrity

We verify physical rebars for Fe550D/Fe600 grades, test structural spacing tolerances, and execute concrete slump tests during structural slab casts. Brickwork alignment is verified with digital vertical laser measures.

Phase 03

Finishes, Plumbing & Waterproofing Audits

A 3-stage pond test (water logged for 72 hours) is mandatory for terraces, utility areas, and underground sumps to ensure 100% leak proofing. Plumbing pressure tests are executed at 10 bar to isolate joint micro-fissures.

Trust Center FAQs

Frequently asked legal, financial, and construction verification questions answered transparently by RCV senior advisors.

How does the TitleGuard™ verification process work?

Our in-house real estate attorneys conduct a thorough 30-year chronological trace of physical records at the sub-registrar's office. We verify mother deeds, check for pending litigations, verify layout plan sanctions, and check revenue records. Only properties with perfect, clean titles are onboarded.

What happens if a construction phase fails QASCON checks?

If a milestone fails to meet our engineering tolerances (e.g. M30 concrete cube crushing strength below standard or slab alignment variation exceeding 3mm), the contractor is immediately issued a rectifying notice. RCV SafeEscrow™ releases zero funds for that phase until the rectifying work is completed and successfully re-audited.

Is RCV SafeEscrow™ safe for NRIs living abroad?

Absolutely. RCV SafeEscrow™ was designed specifically for NRI clients who cannot physically visit building sites. Your money is secured in a corporate escrow account at partner banks. You can monitor drone videos, concrete crush test certificates, and billing receipts in real-time from our dashboard. Releases occur only on your approval.

Are all RCV properties registered under Karnataka RERA?

Yes, 100%. RedCarpet Ventures strictly promotes properties that carry active Karnataka RERA numbers. We verify RERA quarterly filings, project completion extensions, and developer compliance logs before presenting them to our clients.
