Contact & Advisory FAQs

Frequently asked legal, consultation, and portfolio evaluation questions answered transparently by RCV senior advisors.

Is there a fee for the initial advisory consultation?

No. Our initial portfolio appraisal, RCV Build independent villa estimation, and luxury interior spatial planning workshops are 100% free of charge. We only charge structural management fees once project escrow pools are registered.

Can I schedule a physical site visit to properties?

Absolutely. Our micro-market legal advisors arrange door-to-door luxury transport for NRI clients and local executives to explore pre-screened plots, custom independent villas, and premium high-rises in Whitefield, Sarjapur, Devanahalli, Jakkur, and Hebbal.

How do I initiate a TitleGuard™ ancestral title trace?

Simply submit the contact form or register your query on our direct hotline. Our certified lawyers will collect land survey details and pull Kaveri ledgers physically at the sub-registrar office to conduct a chronological title search going back 30 years.

Do you offer corporate advisory for land acquisitions?

Yes. We offer tech-backed property vetting, bulk transaction legal clearances, and joint venture allocations for family offices, NRI funds, and tech enterprises. Our data engines pull ROI metrics directly to check yield spreads.
