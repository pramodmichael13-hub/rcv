Our Six-Stage Building Protocol

How RCV ensures complete structural integrity, budget protection, and exact possession dates throughout your home development.

1. Discovery & Soil Testing

Execute soil bearing capacity testing, structural analysis, and align with top architects to map custom floor plans matching Vastu rules.

2. BOQ Costing Lock

Formulate a granular Bill of Quantities (BOQ) with anti-escalation parameters. Select specifications tier and lock structural budget.

3. Escrow Set Up & BBMP Clearances

Lock built capital inside SafeEscrow™. Secure building permits, BBMP approvals, electrical connections, and mobilize building resources.

4. Structural Framework

Execute earth excavation, plinth casting, framing structures, column casting, and concrete slab layout checked via physical testing compression.

5. Precision MEP & Finishes

Install premium Kohler plumbing, CP teak timber entrance doors, internal conduits, 3-stage waterproofing, and Asian Paints plastering checks.

6. Possession & Warranty

Conduct complete safety clearing, sub-registrar filing, registry validation, and handover of written structural and waterproofing warranties.
